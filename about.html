About MyCryptoDesk

MyCryptoDesk is a lightweight web dashboard that helps users track their favorite cryptocurrencies in real-time. It includes market browsing, live USD and percentage tracking charts, and a personalized favorites system-designed to be fast, visual, and user-friendly.
